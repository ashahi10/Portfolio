About Me

Have you ever cracked a tough problem right before dawn? That's the kind of thrill I live for. I'm pursuing a Bachelor's in Computing Sciences at the University of Alberta, with a minor in Bioinformatics. My academic path has given me a solid foundation in both fields.

As a software developer, I work with languages like Python, Java, and C/C++. I build web applications using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and I'm proficient with frameworks such as TensorFlow, React, Angular, and Django. I enjoy exploring Machine Learning and AI, always looking to expand my knowledge and apply it in creative ways.

Outside of coding, I'm an athlete at heart, always up for a good game. I love staying updated with the latest tech trends, diving into thrilling books, and traveling to new places. These experiences inspire my approach to solving real-world problems with technology. In my free time, I focus on personal projects and skill enhancement.

At Doon Valley International School, where I worked as a Data Analyst, I sharpened my problem-solving skills and learned the importance of data accuracy and teamwork. I gained experience with data analytics tools such as Power BI, SPSS, and Minitab, and performed statistical analysis using MS Excel Pivot Tables and Matlab Statistical Toolbox. I focused on data interpretation, creating detailed reports that shared additional insights to aid in decision-making.

I'm excited to connect with like-minded professionals. Feel free to reach out on LinkedIn or explore my work on GitHub. Let's turn great ideas into reality and make a difference together!
